# Vehium – Mobile Apps (iOS & Android)

> **Vehium is a Garage Management System that connects workshops with their customers in real time.**

> **Our mobile apps are coming soon to the App Store and Google Play worldwide.**

---

## Coming Soon

The Vehium mobile apps are being prepared for a worldwide release on the **Apple App Store** and **Google Play Store**. Once available, you'll be able to download and update them directly from the stores.

### Workshop App

Manage your entire workshop operation from your phone — clients, vehicles, service orders, invoicing, and real-time customer notifications.

- **iOS** — Coming soon to the App Store (requires iOS 15.0+)
- **Android** — Coming soon to Google Play (requires Android 7.0+)

### Customer App

Track your vehicle's service status in real time, receive push notifications, view invoices, and browse your complete vehicle history.

- **iOS** — Coming soon to the App Store (requires iOS 15.0+)
- **Android** — Coming soon to Google Play (requires Android 7.0+)
