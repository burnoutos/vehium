# Vehium – iOS (Customer)

> **Vehium is a Garage Management System that keeps customers notified in real time about what's happening with their vehicle.**

> **This app is distributed via TestFlight and is not yet available on the App Store.**

---

## ⚠️ Before You Install – Please Read

### 1. You Need TestFlight

Vehium for iOS is distributed through **Apple TestFlight**. You must have the TestFlight app installed before you can install Vehium.

- Download **TestFlight** from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/testflight/id899247664) — it's free.

### 2. Accept the Invitation

You will receive an invitation link via email or directly from the Vehium team. Open the link on your iPhone or iPad to accept the invite and install the app through TestFlight.

### 3. No Automatic Updates

TestFlight builds may need to be updated manually. When a new version is available, TestFlight will notify you. Open TestFlight and tap **Update** next to Vehium.

### 4. Build Expiry

TestFlight builds expire after **90 days**. If the app stops working, simply download the latest build from TestFlight or request a new invitation link.

---

## 📦 Installation Steps

1. Install **TestFlight** from the App Store (if not already installed).
2. Open the invitation link sent to you by the Vehium team.
3. Tap **Accept** in TestFlight, then tap **Install**.
4. Once installed, open **Vehium** from your home screen.

---

## 📋 Requirements

- iOS **15.0** or higher
- iPhone or iPad
- ~120 MB free storage
- TestFlight app installed
