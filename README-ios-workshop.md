# Vehium – iOS (Workshop)

> **Vehium is a Garage Management System that allows workshops to manage jobs and notify customers in real time about the status of their vehicle.**

> **This app is distributed via TestFlight and is not yet available on the App Store.**

---

## ⚠️ Before You Install – Please Read

### 1. You Need TestFlight

Vehium Workshop for iOS is distributed through **Apple TestFlight**. You must have the TestFlight app installed before you can install Vehium.

- Download **TestFlight** from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/testflight/id899247664) — it's free.

### 2. Accept the Invitation

You will receive an invitation link from the Vehium team. Open the link on your iPhone or iPad to accept the invite and install the app through TestFlight.

### 3. No Automatic Updates

TestFlight builds may need to be updated manually. When a new version is available, TestFlight will notify you. Open TestFlight and tap **Update** next to Vehium Workshop.

### 4. Build Expiry

TestFlight builds expire after **90 days**. If the app stops working, simply download the latest build from TestFlight or request a new invitation link from the Vehium team.

---

## 📦 Installation Steps

1. Install **TestFlight** from the App Store (if not already installed).
2. Open the invitation link sent to you by the Vehium team.
3. Tap **Accept** in TestFlight, then tap **Install**.
4. Once installed, open **Vehium Workshop** from your home screen.
5. Log in with your workshop credentials provided during onboarding.

---

## 📋 Requirements

- iOS **15.0** or higher
- iPhone or iPad
- ~120 MB free storage
- TestFlight app installed
- Active workshop account on Vehium
